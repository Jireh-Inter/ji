Who We Are

"And Abraham called the name of that place Jehovah-jireh: as it is said to this day, In the mount of the LORD it shall be provided." (Genesis 22:14 ERV)

Jireh means "The Lord will provide" - reflecting our unwavering faith that God will provide for all our needs as we serve communities worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower communities worldwide by building and supporting churches, offering educational scholarships, establishing and supporting orphanages and hospices, and providing financial credit to small-scale businesses—all inspired by Christian principles of love, compassion, and service.

Our Vision

To create thriving, resilient communities where every individual has access to spiritual growth, education, social support, and economic opportunities.

Our Programs

⛪

Church Building & Support

We partner with local communities to construct new churches and renovate existing ones. Our support extends beyond construction to include leadership training, resource provision, and ongoing community development initiatives that strengthen the spiritual foundation of communities.

  • • New church construction
  • • Renovation and maintenance
  • • Leadership training programs
  • • Community outreach support
🎓

Educational Scholarships

Education is the key to breaking cycles of poverty. We provide comprehensive scholarship programs covering tuition, books, and living expenses for deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds, enabling them to pursue primary, secondary, and tertiary education.

  • • Primary school scholarships
  • • Secondary education support
  • • University and college funding
  • • Vocational training programs
🏠

Orphanages & Hospices

We establish and support safe, loving environments for orphaned children and provide dignified care for those in hospice care. Our facilities focus on holistic development, including education, healthcare, spiritual growth, and emotional support.

  • • Orphanage establishment and support
  • • Hospice care facilities
  • • Healthcare and nutrition programs
  • • Emotional and spiritual counseling
💼

Business Microfinance

We empower entrepreneurs and small business owners with accessible microfinance solutions. Our program includes financial literacy training, business mentorship, and ongoing support to help create sustainable livelihoods and economic growth in communities.

  • • Microloans for small businesses
  • • Financial literacy training
  • • Business mentorship programs
  • • Market linkage support
